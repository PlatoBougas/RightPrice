Why pricing today is such a big deal?

Price is one of the most important factors to shoppers, so it's paramount that retailers get it right. While some may think there is an art to pricing, it's one of the most data-driven aspects of retailing, both online and offline. Price optimization is also directly tied to a company's margins. In an October 2018 study by the IRS, retailers in the US said that getting pricing strategy right and executing at a high level is extremely important to financial performance, rating it a 9.2 on a 10-point scale.

Price optimization takes time and effort, but is something that can't be overlooked. Thankfully, tools like Right Price make the process much faster and easier than ever before.
